# Privacy Policy for PetFiles

**Last Updated: January 8, 2026**

**Developer:** Wayward Son Software
**App Name:** PetFiles
**Contact:** owner@waywardsonsoftware.com

## Introduction

PetFiles ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: Email address, name (first and last)
- **Pet Information**: Pet names, species, breeds, birth dates, photos, microchip numbers, weight, and other health-related data
- **Health Records**: Vaccination records, veterinary appointment details, health journal entries, medication information
- **Payment Information**: Managed securely by Google Play Billing (we do not store payment card details)

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version
- **Usage Data**: App features used, session duration
- **Error Logs**: Crash reports and diagnostic data to improve app performance

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and maintain the PetFiles service
- Manage your pet health records
- Send appointment and vaccination reminders (with your permission)
- Process premium subscription payments
- Improve app functionality and user experience
- Respond to your support requests
- Comply with legal obligations

## Data Storage and Security

- **Storage**: Your data is securely stored using Supabase (cloud database service)
- **Encryption**: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS
- **Server Location**: Data is stored on secure servers in [Supabase's data centers]
- **Access Control**: Only you can access your pet health records through your secure account

## Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:
- **Service Providers**: Supabase (database hosting), Google Play (payment processing)
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law or to protect our rights
- **With Your Consent**: When you explicitly authorize sharing

We do not share your data with advertisers or marketing companies.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View all data we store about you
- **Correction**: Update or correct your information
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
- **Export**: Download your pet health records
- **Opt-Out**: Disable notifications and reminders

To exercise these rights, contact us at owner@waywardsonsoftware.com

## Data Retention

- **Active Accounts**: We retain your data as long as your account is active
- **Deleted Accounts**: Data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
- **Backups**: Backup copies are deleted within 90 days

## Children's Privacy

PetArkive is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## Premium Subscription

- **Billing**: Managed through Google Play Billing
- **Payment Data**: We do not store your payment card information
- **Cancellation**: You can cancel your subscription at any time through Google Play
- **Refunds**: Subject to Google Play's refund policy

## Push Notifications

With your permission, we send:
- Vaccination reminders
- Appointment reminders
- Health tracking notifications

You can disable notifications in your device settings at any time.

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:
- **Supabase**: Database and authentication (see [Supabase Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy))
- **Google Play Services**: Payment processing and app distribution
- **Expo**: App development framework

Continued use of PetFiles after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
